Welcome!

This website has been created to inform and educate people when it comes to their health. We strive for people to have a platform where they can access critical information on health issues happening around you. This website specifically looks into Boston's most occuring illnesses and death causes and least occuring death causes to give people an idea on what is killing people and making people sick around them. All graphs were created by using the MIMIC III Database.

Start off by using the top of the page to explore more about Deaths and Illnesses in your area.
